Expand description

Trait providing the base algorithm for flowing fluids (Water, Lava). In vanilla Minecraft, this is the FlowingFluid abstract class.

The base logic for placing a fluid into a specific adjacent block.

Vanilla equivalent: FlowingFluid.spreadTo(). Note: vanilla’s spreadTo does NOT schedule ticks — that’s handled by LiquidBlockContainer.placeLiquid or the new block’s onPlace callback.

fn spread_to_sides( &self, world: &Arc<World>, pos: BlockPos, fluid_state: FluidState, )

Spreads the fluid to horizontal neighbors.

Vanilla equivalent: FlowingFluid.spreadToSides(). Computes outgoing amount, overrides to 7 for falling fluids, and skips if the outgoing amount is zero.
