Matches vanilla’s iterateNoiseColumn: iterates by Y cells, evaluating inner density functions at 8 cell corners, trilinearly interpolating each channel independently, then applying outer operations (squeeze, min, etc.) per-block via combine_interpolated.

Returns getBaseHeight (= getFirstFreeHeight = first Y above surface).
